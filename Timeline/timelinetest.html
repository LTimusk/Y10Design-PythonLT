1970s

This is an NBA shot chart for various shots taken by Bucks/Lakers player Kareem Abdul-Jabbar, in various games from 1969 to 1980. Throughout these games, the first thing we notice about the shots he took was that they were almost all from within 10 feet of the basket. He took these shots because they were highly efficient looks, and he made them consistently.
